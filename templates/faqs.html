Frequently Asked Questions

Browsing and Shopping

Do I need to register to purchase?

Yes, you need to create an account to complete a purchase. Registration is quick and simple, requiring just your email address and basic information. Having an account allows you to track your orders and save your favorite items.

I'm a Discount Cardholder, do I get any discounts?

Yes! Discount Cardholders receive 10% off all regular-priced items. Simply log in to your account with your Discount Card credentials to automatically apply your discount at checkout.

How do I browse for books?

You can browse books by category using our navigation menu, use the search bar to find specific titles or authors, or check out our curated collections and recommendations on the homepage.

How do I add items to my Shopping Cart?

Simply click the "Add to Cart" button on any product page. You can view and edit your cart at any time by clicking the cart icon in the top right corner of the page.

What happens in Checkout?

During checkout, you'll confirm your items, enter shipping information, select a shipping method, enter payment details, and finally review and place your order.

Payment and Processing

How do I pay? What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I pay Cash on Delivery (COD)?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for orders within select areas. Additional fees may apply for this payment method.

What happens after I've paid? Do I get a notification?

After payment, you'll receive an order confirmation email with your order details. You'll also receive shipping updates once your order is processed and shipped.

Why do the prices of some items change?

Prices may change due to publisher adjustments, promotional discounts, or limited-time offers. We always display the current price on the product page.
